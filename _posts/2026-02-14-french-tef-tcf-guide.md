## üéØ What is the "Magic Number" (CLB 7)?

To get the 50 bonus points, you need **NCLC 7 (CLB 7)**.
Here is what that looks like in raw scores:

* **Listening:** 249-279 (TEF) / 458-502 (TCF)
* **Reading:** 207-232 (TEF) / 453-498 (TCF)
* **Writing:** 310-348 (TEF) / 10-11 (TCF)
* **Speaking:** 310-348 (TEF) / 10-11 (TCF)

**Can a beginner do it?**
Yes. If you start from Zero (A1), it takes about **8-10 months** of dedicated study (10 hours/week) to reach B2 (which is roughly CLB 7).
