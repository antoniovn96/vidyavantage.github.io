<h2 id="final-verdict">üèÅ Final Verdict: Which one should YOU take?</h2>
<p>Choosing between the two depends entirely on your test-taking strengths:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Take TEF if:</strong> You are highly analytical, good at reading long texts, and comfortable acting out a persuasive roleplay (excellent for Lawyers, Sales pros, and MBA grads).</li>
  <li><strong>Take TCF if:</strong> You prefer straightforward Q&A formats, have strong, quick listening skills, and are worried about the anxiety of negative marking.</li>
