# The "Coaching Mafia" Myth.

Every year, Indian students spend crores on IELTS coaching.
**Here is the truth:** IELTS is not a test of your *intelligence* (like JEE). It is a test of your *familiarity* with English.

If you watch Netflix without subtitles and can write a decent email, you can score Band 8 **on your own**. You just need a strategy.

## üéØ Academic vs. General Training: Which one?

Don't book the wrong exam!

| Feature | **IELTS Academic** | **IELTS General Training** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Who is it for?** | Students (Undergrad/Masters) | Working Professionals (PR Visa) |
| **Reading Section** | Long, complex academic passages | Short texts, ads, notices |
| **Writing Task 1** | Describe a Graph/Chart | Write a Letter (Formal/Informal) |
| **Difficulty** | High | Medium |

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes that Kill Your Score



1.  **Leaving blanks empty:** There is NO negative marking in IELTS. Guess if you don't know!
2.  **Writing too little:** If Task 2 asks for 250 words, writing 240 will lower your score penalty. Aim for 260-280.
3.  **Memorizing answers:** Examiners are trained to spot "scripted" answers in the Speaking test.
