# Apples vs Oranges

* **JEE:** Depth. They test if you know Physics better than Einstein.
* **SAT:** Speed. They test if you can do basic Math fast and read English correctly.

**Verdict:** If you are creative and well-rounded, take the SAT. If you are technically brilliant, stick to JEE.
