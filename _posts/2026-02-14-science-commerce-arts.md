# The Myth of "Scope"

Parents say "Science has scope." But did you know a **Corporate Lawyer (Arts stream)** often earns more than an average **IT Engineer** in Bangalore today?

In 2026, "Scope" is not about the stream. It is about **Excellence**. An excellent Graphic Designer makes more money than an average Doctor.
