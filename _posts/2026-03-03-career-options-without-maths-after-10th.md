<h3 style="color: #0A2342; font-size: 1.2rem;">Can I get a high-paying job without Maths?</h3>
<p>Yes, absolutely. Careers in corporate law, UI/UX design, clinical psychology, hotel management, and digital marketing pay exceptionally well and do not require higher mathematics.</p>

<h3 style="color: #0A2342; font-size: 1.2rem;">Is Commerce possible without Maths?</h3>
<p>Yes. Many reputed schools offer Commerce with alternative subjects like Informatics Practices (IP), Entrepreneurship, or Physical Education instead of Mathematics.</p>

<h3 style="color: #0A2342; font-size: 1.2rem;">Which stream is best if I hate Maths?</h3>
<p>Arts / Humanities is the safest and most diverse stream. Alternatively, Commerce without Maths is an excellent choice if you want to enter the corporate world.</p>
