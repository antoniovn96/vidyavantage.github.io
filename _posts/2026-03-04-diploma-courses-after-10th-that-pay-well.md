<h2 id="what-are-diplomas">What Are Diploma Courses After 10th?</h2>
<p>Diploma courses are skill-oriented professional programs that focus on intense practical training rather than academic theory. They are designed to make students "industry-ready" faster.</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Duration:</strong> 2–3 years.</li>
  <li><strong>Curriculum:</strong> Industry-focused and hands-on.</li>
  <li><strong>Outcome:</strong> Early job opportunities and technical expertise.</li>
  <li><strong>Flexible Path:</strong> Option to pursue a degree later via Lateral Entry (directly into 2nd year B.Tech).</li>
</ul>
