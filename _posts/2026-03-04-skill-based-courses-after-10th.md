<h2 id="what-are-skill-courses">What Are Skill-Based Courses?</h2>
<p>Skill-based courses (often called Vocational Courses or short-term Diplomas) are programs designed to teach practical, job-ready abilities instead of only theoretical knowledge. Due to massive government initiatives like the <em>Skill India Mission</em>, these courses are highly respected and heavily funded.</p>

<p><strong>Key Features:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Heavy focus on hands-on lab or studio training.</li>
  <li>Curriculum dictated by current industry needs, not outdated textbooks.</li>
  <li>Shorter duration (6 months to 2 years).</li>
  <li>Much faster employment and entrepreneurship opportunities.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="color: #0A2342; font-size: 1.2rem;">Are skill-based courses good after 10th?</h3>
<p>Yes. They are exceptional for students who want to bypass heavy theory, gain practical training, and enter the workforce faster to start earning an income.</p>

<h3 style="color: #0A2342; font-size: 1.2rem;">Can I earn well through skill-based careers?</h3>
<p>Absolutely. Skilled professionals in the digital (SEO, Web Development) and creative (UI/UX, Animation) industries frequently earn higher incomes than standard graduates, especially as freelancers or agency owners.</p>

<h3 style="color: #0A2342; font-size: 1.2rem;">Can I continue higher education later?</h3>
<p>Yes. Many skill courses and diplomas offer "Lateral Entry" options or pathways to pursue specialized degrees later if you choose to re-enter academia.</p>
